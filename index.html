Satified Study

— Mirza N. Baig

I built this SAT platform to provide 1,000+ carefully designed math questions that target the exact patterns and problem types the College Board uses on the SAT Math section. Every question is intentional and focused to capture almost every single concept the SAT could throw at you.

This is not a site for learning basic algebra or geometry. Resources like Khan Academy already do that well.

The issue is that many platforms do not fully match the difficulty and structure of real SAT math questions — especially at medium and hard levels. And the ones that come closer, like Princeton Review, are not always free.

There is also a lack of true variety. Many sites repeat similar problems with small changes. My platform includes multiple regenerations of each question type so you can practice the same concept repeatedly with different numbers and setups, helping you master patterns rather than memorize answers.
